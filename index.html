    Alpha+ Analytical Engine

    Quantitative Trading Intelligence

    Momentum (RSI)
    Monitors Relative Strength Index to identify overbought (>70) and oversold (<30) market conditions.
    Trend (MACD)
    Uses Moving Average Convergence Divergence to detect momentum acceleration and trend reversals.
    Volatility (BB)
    Applies Bollinger Bands (20-day SMA + 2SD) to visualize price envelopes and volatility squeezes.
    Full Stack Architecture: HTML5/CSS3 • Vanilla JavaScript • Google Apps Script (GAS) • Finnhub API
